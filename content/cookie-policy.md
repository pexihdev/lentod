# Cookie Policy

_Last updated: July 11, 2026_

Welcome to **Lentod**.

This Cookie Policy explains how Lentod uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website.

By continuing to use our website, you agree to the use of cookies as described in this policy, unless you disable them through your browser settings.

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser.

They help websites remember information about your visit, making your browsing experience faster, more secure, and more personalized.

Cookies do not normally contain personally identifiable information, although they may be associated with information you voluntarily provide.

## Why We Use Cookies

Lentod uses cookies for several purposes, including:

- Improving website performance.
- Remembering user preferences.
- Enhancing security.
- Understanding how visitors use our website.
- Measuring website traffic.
- Supporting website functionality.
- Detecting technical problems.
- Preventing abuse and malicious activity.

## Types of Cookies We Use

### Essential Cookies

These cookies are required for the website to function properly.

They enable core functionality such as:

- Navigation
- Security
- Session management
- Basic website features

Without these cookies, some parts of the website may not function correctly.

### Preference Cookies

These cookies remember your preferences, including:

- Theme selection
- Language preferences
- Display settings
- Other user preferences

### Analytics Cookies

Analytics cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website.

Information collected may include:

- Pages visited
- Visit duration
- Referral sources
- Device type
- Browser type
- Operating system

This information is generally aggregated and does not directly identify individual users.

### Performance Cookies

These cookies help us improve website speed, reliability, and user experience.

They allow us to identify slow pages and optimize website performance.

### Advertising Cookies

If advertising is enabled on Lentod, third-party advertising providers may use cookies to display advertisements that are more relevant to your interests.

Advertising cookies may collect information about browsing behavior across websites.

Third-party advertising providers maintain their own privacy policies.

## Third-Party Cookies

Some third-party services integrated into Lentod may place cookies on your device.

Examples may include:

- Analytics providers
- Advertising networks
- Embedded videos
- Social media platforms
- Content delivery services

Lentod does not control cookies placed by third-party services.

Please review the privacy policies of those providers for additional information.

## Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to:

- View stored cookies.
- Delete cookies.
- Block cookies.
- Disable cookies.
- Configure cookie preferences.

Please note that disabling cookies may affect certain website features and functionality.

## Browser Settings

You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

Most modern browsers provide options to:

- Accept all cookies.
- Reject all cookies.
- Delete existing cookies.
- Notify you before cookies are stored.

Refer to your browser's documentation for instructions.

## Do Not Track

Some browsers support a "Do Not Track" (DNT) setting.

Currently, there is no universal standard governing how websites should respond to DNT signals.

Therefore, Lentod may continue to operate normally regardless of your browser's DNT setting.

## Data Protection

Cookies themselves generally do not contain sensitive personal information.

Any personal information associated with cookies is handled in accordance with our Privacy Policy.

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect legal, technical, or operational changes.

Updated versions will be published on this page with a revised effective date.
