# Editorial Policy

_Last updated: July 11, 2026_

Welcome to **Lentod**.

Our Editorial Policy explains the principles and standards that guide the creation, review, publication, and maintenance of content on Lentod.

Our goal is to provide accurate, useful, transparent, and trustworthy technology content for readers worldwide.

## Our Mission

Lentod is committed to publishing educational and informative content covering topics such as:

- Software Development
- Artificial Intelligence
- Cloud Computing
- Cybersecurity
- Linux
- DevOps
- Open Source Software
- Web Development
- Mobile Development
- Programming Languages
- APIs and Frameworks
- Technology News

Our mission is to help readers learn, build, and make informed technology decisions.

## Editorial Principles

Every article published on Lentod is guided by the following principles:

- Accuracy
- Transparency
- Fairness
- Independence
- Technical correctness
- Reader-first approach

We strive to ensure our content is clear, objective, and easy to understand.

## Research Process

Before publishing an article, we aim to:

- Research the topic thoroughly.
- Verify technical information.
- Review official documentation where applicable.
- Test examples whenever practical.
- Update outdated information when necessary.

Technology changes rapidly, and we continuously improve our published content.

## Sources

Whenever appropriate, we rely on reputable sources, including:

- Official documentation
- Open-source project documentation
- Standards organizations
- Vendor documentation
- Public technical references
- Original research

We avoid relying solely on rumors or unverified claims.

## Editorial Independence

Our editorial decisions are made independently.

Advertising, sponsorships, affiliate relationships, or commercial partnerships do not determine our editorial opinions.

Sponsored content, if published, will be clearly identified.

## Accuracy

Although we strive for accuracy, mistakes may occasionally occur.

If an error is identified, we will review and correct it as appropriate.

Readers are encouraged to report inaccuracies through our Contact page.

## AI-Assisted Content

Some content may be created or improved with the assistance of artificial intelligence tools.

All AI-assisted content is reviewed before publication to improve accuracy, readability, and usefulness.

Human editorial oversight remains an important part of our publishing process.

## Product Reviews

Product reviews reflect our evaluation at the time of publication.

Products and services may change after publication.

Readers should perform their own research before making purchasing decisions.

## Security Content

Security-related articles are published for educational and defensive purposes.

We do not encourage illegal activity, unauthorized access, or malicious use of technology.

Readers remain responsible for complying with all applicable laws.

## User Feedback

We welcome constructive feedback.

Suggestions, corrections, and comments help improve the quality of our content.

## Content Updates

Articles may be updated to:

- Correct errors.
- Improve clarity.
- Reflect software updates.
- Add new information.
- Remove outdated content.

Updated articles may include revised publication dates where appropriate.

## Conflicts of Interest

When applicable, we aim to disclose relationships that could reasonably be perceived as influencing editorial content.

Transparency helps maintain reader trust.

## Editorial Responsibility

The editorial team works to ensure published content aligns with the standards described in this policy.

Despite our efforts, no publication can guarantee that every article will remain permanently accurate as technology evolves.

## Changes to This Policy

This Editorial Policy may be updated periodically.

Any changes become effective upon publication of the revised version on this page.

## Contact

Questions regarding this Editorial Policy may be submitted through our Contact page.

Thank you for reading Lentod.
