# DMCA Policy

_Last updated: July 11, 2026_

Lentod respects the intellectual property rights of others and expects users, contributors, and visitors to do the same.

This Digital Millennium Copyright Act ("DMCA") Policy explains how copyright owners may notify us of alleged copyright infringement and how we respond to such notices.

## Copyright Respect

We make every reasonable effort to ensure that content published on Lentod either:

- Is our original work;
- Is used with permission;
- Is available under an appropriate open-source or public license; or
- Qualifies for lawful use under applicable copyright laws.

If you believe your copyrighted work has been used improperly on Lentod, please notify us.

## Filing a Copyright Notice

Your copyright notice should include:

- Your full name.
- Your contact information.
- A description of the copyrighted work.
- The exact URL(s) where the allegedly infringing material appears.
- A statement that you have a good-faith belief the use is not authorized.
- A statement that the information provided is accurate.
- A statement made under penalty of perjury that you are the copyright owner or authorized to act on behalf of the owner.
- Your physical or electronic signature.

Incomplete notices may delay processing.

## Review Process

After receiving a valid copyright notice, we may:

- Review the reported material.
- Request additional information if necessary.
- Temporarily disable access to the content.
- Remove infringing material if appropriate.
- Contact the content contributor when applicable.

Each report is reviewed individually.

## Counter Notification

If you believe content was removed in error, you may submit a counter notification containing:

- Your name and contact information.
- Identification of the removed material.
- A statement under penalty of perjury that you believe the material was removed due to mistake or misidentification.
- Consent to the jurisdiction of the appropriate court where applicable.
- Your physical or electronic signature.

We will review every counter notification in accordance with applicable law.

## Repeat Infringers

Lentod reserves the right to restrict or terminate access for users or contributors who repeatedly violate copyright laws.

## False Claims

Submitting false or misleading copyright claims may result in legal consequences under applicable law.

Please ensure all submitted information is truthful and accurate.

## Open Source Software

Many code examples published on Lentod reference open-source software.

Each project remains subject to its own license.

Users must comply with the applicable license terms of any referenced software.

## Trademarks

All trademarks, logos, product names, and brand names mentioned on Lentod remain the property of their respective owners.

Their appearance does not imply endorsement unless explicitly stated.

## Contact

To report copyright concerns, please contact us through the Contact page and include all relevant information required for review.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this DMCA Policy from time to time.

Any revisions will become effective immediately upon publication on this page.

Thank you for helping us protect intellectual property rights.
