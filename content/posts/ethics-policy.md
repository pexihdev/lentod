# Ethics Policy

_Last updated: July 11, 2026_

At **Lentod**, we believe trust is built through honesty, transparency, responsibility, and respect.

This Ethics Policy outlines the principles that guide our content creation, publishing practices, and interactions with our community.

## Our Core Values

Lentod follows these core values:

- Accuracy
- Transparency
- Fairness
- Responsibility
- Respect
- Continuous improvement

We aim to create content that provides real value to our readers.

## Accuracy and Verification

We strive to ensure that published information is accurate and reliable.

Before publishing content, we aim to:

- Verify important facts.
- Review technical information.
- Use reliable sources.
- Avoid spreading unconfirmed claims.
- Update outdated information.

## Honesty and Transparency

We are committed to being transparent about:

- Sources of information.
- Sponsored content.
- Affiliate relationships.
- Product reviews.
- AI-assisted content when relevant.

Readers should always be able to understand the context behind our content.

## Editorial Independence

Our editorial decisions are independent.

Commercial relationships, advertising, sponsorships, or partnerships do not control our opinions or editorial conclusions.

We maintain the right to publish honest evaluations and analysis.

## Responsible Technology Reporting

Technology can have significant impacts on society.

When discussing technology, cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, or software, we aim to:

- Provide responsible information.
- Avoid encouraging harmful activities.
- Consider security and privacy implications.
- Promote ethical technology use.

## Artificial Intelligence Usage

AI tools may be used to assist with:

- Research organization.
- Draft improvement.
- Translation.
- Content formatting.
- Idea generation.

However, AI-generated information must be reviewed and verified before publication.

Human oversight remains essential.

## Cybersecurity Ethics

Lentod supports responsible cybersecurity practices.

We do not promote:

- Unauthorized access.
- Malware creation.
- Data theft.
- Abuse of systems.
- Illegal activities.

Security topics are published for educational and defensive purposes.

## Respect for Intellectual Property

We respect:

- Copyright ownership.
- Software licenses.
- Open-source licenses.
- Creative works.
- Trademarks.

We expect contributors and users to respect intellectual property rights.

## Fair Representation

We aim to represent technologies, products, companies, and individuals fairly.

We avoid:

- Misleading claims.
- Unnecessary criticism.
- Biased reporting.
- Manipulated information.

## User Privacy

Respecting user privacy is a fundamental principle.

We handle user information according to our Privacy Policy and applicable regulations.

## Corrections and Accountability

If we publish incorrect information, we will make reasonable efforts to correct it.

We welcome feedback from readers who help us improve our content.

## Community Standards

We encourage respectful discussion.

We do not support content that promotes:

- Harassment.
- Hate.
- Discrimination.
- Violence.
- Illegal activities.
- Harmful behavior.

## Conflict of Interest

Writers and contributors should disclose relationships that could influence content.

Transparency helps maintain reader trust.

## Continuous Improvement

Technology and publishing standards continue to evolve.

We regularly review our practices to improve the quality, reliability, and responsibility of Lentod.

## Changes to This Policy

This Ethics Policy may be updated periodically.

Changes become effective when published on this page.

## Contact

Questions or concerns regarding this Ethics Policy may be submitted through our Contact page.

Thank you for being part of the Lentod community.
