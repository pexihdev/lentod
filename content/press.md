# Press & Media

## About Lentod

**Lentod** is a technology platform focused on sharing knowledge, tutorials, guides, and resources about modern digital technologies.

Our content covers areas including:

- Artificial intelligence
- Software development
- Cloud computing
- Cybersecurity
- Open source
- Web technologies
- Developer tools
- Programming

Our goal is to make complex technology easier to understand and accessible to a wider audience.
